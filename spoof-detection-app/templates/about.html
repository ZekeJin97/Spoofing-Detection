What’s This?

A playful yet powerful app that spots deepfakes in your photos. It fuses multiple clues—texture, depth, facial patches, and edge boundaries—into a single brainy MLP to call “REAL” or “FAKE.”

Why It’s Cool

  • 🔍 Texture & Edges: CLAHE + Sobel filters with ResNet18
  • 👁️ Patches: Eyes, nose, mouth & more via MediaPipe
  • 🕳️ Depth: MiDaS transformer for 3D cues
  • 🧩 Boundary: Contour consistency to catch blending tricks
  • 🚀 One-Click: Upload & get verdict + confidence
